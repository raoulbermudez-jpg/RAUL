# GSPT Submersible Pump Total Protection Relay

## Overview

Relayne GSPT is a microcontrolled based three-phase Motor Protection Relay, specifically designed to protect electric loads and motors from failure and damage due to common current and voltage faults.

## Integral Measurement of

- Current
- Voltage
- Frequency
- Power Factor (PF)
- Reactive Power (KVA)
- Real Power (KW)
- Energy Consumption (KWH)
- Temperature

## Protection Against

- Overload / Undercurrent
- Overvoltage / Undercurrent
- Frequency Shift
- Voltage Unbalance
- Current Unbalance
- Single Phasing
- Phase Reversal
- Locked Rotor

## Key Features

### Protection Capabilities

Relayne GSPT constantly supervises current and voltage values; when any harmful condition occurs, the output connection is deactivated until the fault disappears, power line conditions return to an acceptable level and the motor has been totally cooled.

### Adjustable Parameters

- Overload
- Undercurrent
- Overvoltage
- Undervoltage
- Current Unbalance
- Voltage Unbalance
- Frequency
- Trip Delay
- Start Up Delay after Voltage Fault
- Timer to connection after Current Overload restore
- AUTO / MANUAL Restart Mode

### Submersible Pump Protection

GSPT increases protection for submersible pumps by limiting the amount of allowed starting during continuous hours of operation according to the capacity of the motor given in HP power. It protects continuously against events of Locked Rotor Accelerated and provides adjustable timing to connection after overload.

- 16x2 backlight LCD Display with current values, voltage values and load report information on screen

  - 2 for ADJUST
