# ELECTRONIC SURGE PROTECTOR

## General Protection Features

- Protection against low and high voltage
- Protection against blackouts
- Internal timed connection
- Ground connection
- Light indicators for High Voltage, Low Voltage, Time Delay and Normal Voltage
- Easy installation (in only 3 steps)
- Compact size

## Technical Specifications

| Specification | Value |
|---|---|
| Nominal Voltage | 120 V-, 50/60 Hz |
| Normal Range | 90 V - 135 V- |
| Failure detection time | 0.5s |
| Connection time | 30s |
| Max. Output | 12A, 1/3HP, 1440W |
| Operating Temperature | -5 to 55°C |
| Maximum operating humidity | 85% H.R. |

## Operation

### Normal Voltage
If the voltage is between 90 and 135V-, no failure light will turn on and a 30s timer will start, turning on the Delay time light. Once the 30s have passed, the Delay time light will turn off, and the Normal voltage light will turn on, making sure the voltage is within the normal range.

### High Voltage Detection
If the voltage is higher than 135 V-, the High Voltage light indicator will turn on, and the protected appliance will turn off. If the voltage is exactly 135 V-, the High Voltage light and the Time delay light indicators will turn on simultaneously, and the protected appliance will turn off.

### Low Voltage Detection
If the voltage is lower than 90 V-, the Low Voltage light indicator will turn on, and the protected appliance will turn off. If the voltage is exactly 90 V-, the Low Voltage and the Time Delay light indicators will turn on simultaneously, and the protected appliance will turn off.

## Light Indicators

- Normal Voltage
- Time Delay (30 sec)
- High Voltage
- Low Voltage

## Recommended For

- Front and top load washers
- Gas dryers
- Small appliances
- LED / LCD / PLASMA TVs
- Sound equipments
- Microwaves
- Electronics

## Not Recommended For

Refrigerators, freezers, air conditioners. For these applications, see models GSM-NP and GSM-RE 220.

## Installation Instructions

1. Plug the Exceline Protector into the receptacle
2. The protector will indicate Low Voltage for 2s, then the Time Delay will start
3. The normal Voltage light indicator will turn on after 30s

## Safety Warnings

- CHECK YOUR PLUG AND SOCKET. If they are loose or in bad condition it will generate a bad connection and can damage the protector and the appliance
- RISK OF ELECTRIC SHOCK. Before any repair or maintenance by qualified technical personnel, you must unplug the protector and the appliance to protect
- FOR YOUR SAFETY and for warranty purposes, do not exceed the maximum capacity
