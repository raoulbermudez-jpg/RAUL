# Exceline Voltage Monitor & Surge Protector for Washers & Small Appliances

## Easy Installation Features

- Protection against Low and High Voltage
- Protection against blackouts
- Surge suppressor
- Time delay connection of 30sec
- Ground connection
- Light indicators for High Voltage, Low Voltage, Time Delay and Normal Voltage
- Easy Installation
- Compact size

## Operation

### Spike Suppressor
In case the power supply accidentally brings a Voltage Spike, the protector will absorb it, reducing the risk of internal damage in your equipment; protecting the electronic circuits.

### Normal Voltage
If the voltage is between 90 and 135V~, no failure light will turn on and a 30sec. timer will start, turning on the Time Delay light. The appliance will turn on only after the 30sec time delay has elapsed and the Normal voltage light will turn on, making sure the voltage is within the normal range.

### High Voltage Detection
If the voltage is higher than 135V~, the High Voltage light indicator will turn on, and the protected appliance will turn off.

### Low Voltage Detection
If the voltage is lower than 90V~, the Low Voltage light indicator will turn on, and the protected appliance will turn off.

## Installation Instructions

1. Plug the appliance into the Exceline protector.
2. Plug the Exceline Protector into the receptacle.
3. The protector will indicate Low Voltage for 2sec, then the Time Delay will start and the normal Voltage light indicator will turn on after 30sec.

**NOTE: ONCE YOU PLUG IN THE PROTECTOR, IT WILL TAKE 30 SEC. TO TURN YOUR EQUIPMENT ON (DELAY CYCLE, YELLOW LIGHT).**
