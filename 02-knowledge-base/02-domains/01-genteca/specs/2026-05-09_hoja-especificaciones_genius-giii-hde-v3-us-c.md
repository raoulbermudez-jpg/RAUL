## Communications

- GIO Port or RS485 @ 9600 baud
- MODBUS RTU protocol available (*)
- Digital Output Status
- Remote On/Off

(*) GIO port requires an additional accessory.

## Mounting Options

An innovative mechanical design allows three (3) placement options:

- Symmetrical Din-Rail mounting
- Flat Surface mounting using an exclusive attachable mounting ear
- Flush mounting

## Performance Range

- Input: IN +/- 60%
- Output: OUT +/- 50%

## Installation and Maintenance

Only qualified technicians with knowledge about overload protection relay and associated machinery should do the installation, start-up, and maintenance of the system. Failure to comply may result in equipment damage and/or personal injury.

## Environmental Notice

This product has been designed for industrial environment. Use of this product in residential environment may cause unwanted electromagnetic disturbances in which case the user may be required to take adequate mitigation measures.
