# GII+ Phase Voltage Relay

## Product Overview

The GII+ is a phase voltage relay designed for industrial electrical protection applications. It is manufactured and designed by Genteca (Gente, Generación de Tecnología C.A.).

## Communications

- **GIO Port**: MODBUS RTU protocol
- **Baud Rate**: 9600
- **Configuration**: 8N1
- **Remote Control**: On/Off capability via communications

## Physical Features

### Components
- LCD Screen for display and monitoring
- Indicator Lights (LED's) for operational status
- Select Push Buttons for configuration
- Adjust Push Button for parameter adjustment
- Start Push Buttons for operation control
- GIO Port for communications (MODBUS RTU)
- Trip Relay (Control relay)
- Attachable Mounting Ears
- Flush Mounting Bracket and Attachment options

### Materials
- PC (Polycarbonate)
- ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene)
- Nylon

## Electrical Connections

- **Voltage Input**: Three-phase (L1, L2, L3)
- **Trip Relay**: Open/Closed contact configurations

## Optional Accessories

- GIO Plug (adaptador opcional) - for communications connection

## Safety and Operating Conditions

**Important**: Only qualified technicians with knowledge of voltage protection relays and associated machinery should perform installation, startup, and maintenance of the system. Failure to comply may result in equipment damage and/or personal injury.

**Environmental Notice**: This product has been designed for industrial environments. Use in residential environments may cause unwanted electromagnetic disturbances, in which case adequate mitigation measures may be required.
