# GSM-AS Series Air Conditioning Controls

## GSM-AS120BE

**Input Voltage:** 120 V~ 60 Hz  
**Cooling Capacity:** 18,000 BTU

### Connection Diagram
- **Input:** Ground, N, L
- **Output:** Ground, N, S

### Operating Parameters

| Parameter | Minimum | Maximum | Suggested Range |
|-----------|---------|---------|-----------------|
| Reconnection Timer | 165 | 260 | 195–240 |

## GSM-AS220BE

**Input Voltage:** 220 V~ 60 Hz  
**Cooling Capacity:** 48,000 BTU

### Connection Diagram
- **Input:** Ground, L1, L2
- **Output:** Ground, L2, S

### Operating Parameters

| Parameter | Minimum | Maximum | Suggested Range |
|-----------|---------|---------|-----------------|
| Reconnection Timer | 175 | 280 | 220–260 |
| Additional Range | 205 | 300 | 200–260 |
| Alternative Range | 230 | - | 180–240 |
