# Exceline Voltage Monitor & Electronic Surge Protector
## GSM-MT120CE - Easy Installation (Only 3 Steps)

## Operation

### Normal Voltage
When the voltage is within the Normal range 90-140 VAC any indication of failure will disappear. The Time Delay indicator YELLOW LIGHT will turn on for 30 sec. After the 30 sec the Normal Voltage indicator GREEN LIGHT will turn on if there is any failure and if the voltage is stable. When the Normal Voltage Light is ON the 6 outlets are energized if the ON/OFF switch is ON.

### High and Low Voltage Detection
If the voltage is over or under the normal range the Failure in the Voltage indicator RED LIGHT will turn ON and all the outlets disconnected to protect your devices.

### ON/OFF Switch with Overload Protection
Maximum capacity is 15A, 1800 W. The total amount of devices amperage should not be more than 15 A. In case of an overload or short circuit the switch will disconnect the outlets. This is a Voltage and Surge protector only for Electronic devices. Not to be used for electrical tools, motors, washer, hair dryer, vacuums, microwaves, toaster, or ovens.

### Protection Against Instant Spikes
In presence of spikes the internal surge suppressor will absorb the energy. The Light Spikes Suppressor (Rayo) should be ON to verify that the surge suppressor is functional within its capacity to absorb the spikes. If this light does not turn ON it is an indicative that its useful life has already been completed. This fact does not apply for warranty purposes because the device fulfilled the protection function.

### Internal EMI Suppressor Filter
In case of the presence of electromagnetic noise interferes in the electrical grid the internal EMI suppressor will reduce it.

## Installation Instructions

1. Plug in all the devices
2. Turn the manual switch ON
3. For two seconds it will indicate Voltage Failure, then the Time Delay will start, and the Normal Voltage and the Suppressor Light will turn ON

## Important Notes

- CHECK YOUR PLUG AND SOCKET: If they are loose or in bad condition it will generate a bad connection and the Protector and the devices could be damaged
- RISK OF ELECTRIC SHOCK: Before any repair or maintenance by qualified technical personal, you must unplug the protector and the devices
- FOR YOUR SAFETY and warranty purposes, do not exceed the maximum capacity
