# Exceline Voltage Monitor & Electronic Surge Protector

## Easy Installation (Only 3 Steps)

1. Plug in all the devices
2. Turn the manual switch ON
3. For two seconds it will indicate Voltage Failure, then the Time Delay will start, the Normal Voltage and the Suppressor Light will turn ON

## Operation

### Normal Voltage
When the voltage is within the Normal range 90-140 VAC any indication of failure will disappear. Then the Time Delay indicator YELLOW LIGHT will turn on for 30 sec. After the 30 sec the Normal Voltage indicator GREEN LIGHT will turn on, if there is any failure or if the voltage is stable. When the Normal Voltage Light is ON the 6 outlets are energized if the ON/OFF switch is ON.

### High and Low Voltage Detection
If the voltage is over or under the normal range the Failure in the Voltage indicator RED LIGHT will turn ON and all the outlets will be disconnected to protect your devices.

### ON/OFF Switch with Overload Protection
Remove the central screw from the wall outlet. In case of an overload or short circuit the switch will disconnect the outlets. The maximum capacity is 15A, 1800W. The total amount of devices amperage should not be more than 15A. This is a Voltage and Surge protector only for Electronic devices. Not to be used for electrical tools, motors, washer, hair dryer, vacuums, microwaves, toaster, or ovens.

### Protection Against Instant Spikes
In presence of spikes the internal surge suppressor will absorb the energy. To verify that the surge suppressor is functional within its capacity to absorb the spikes the Light Spikes Suppressor should be ON. If this light does not turn ON it is an indicative that its useful life has already been completed. This fact does not apply for warranty purposes because the device fulfilled the protection function.

### Internal EMI Suppressor Filter
In case of electromagnetic noise interferes in the electrical grid the internal EMI suppressor will reduce it.
