# GCM-F125 Moulded Case Circuit Breaker - 2 Pole Series

## Product Overview

The GCM-F125 series is a line of moulded case circuit breakers (MCCB) with 2-pole configuration. All models comply with IEC/EN 60947-2 standard and are manufactured and distributed by GENTE (Generación de Tecnología).

## Physical Specifications

- **Width:** 45 mm
- **Height:** 73 mm
- **Depth:** 37 mm
