## Communications

- **GIO Port** or **RS485 @ 9600 baud** (MODBUS RTU protocol)
- GIO port requires an additional accessory
- Digital Output Status available
- Remote On/Off capability

## Mounting Options

An innovative mechanical design allows three (3) placement options:
- Symmetrical Din-Rail mounting
- Flat Surface mounting using an exclusive attachable mounting ear
- Flush mounting

## Specifications

- **Input Range:** IN +/- 60%
- **Output Range:** OUT +/- 50%

## Operating Conditions

- Designed for industrial environment
- Use in residential environment may cause unwanted electromagnetic disturbances requiring adequate mitigation measures

## Safety Notice

Only qualified technicians with knowledge about overload protection relay and associated machinery should perform installation, start-up, and maintenance of the system. Failure to comply may result in equipment damage and/or personal injury.
