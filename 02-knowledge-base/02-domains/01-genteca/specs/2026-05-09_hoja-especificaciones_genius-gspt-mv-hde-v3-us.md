# GSPT-MV Submersible Pump Total Protection Relay

## Product Overview

Relayne GSPT-MV is a microcontrolled based three-phase Total Motor Protection Relay, specifically designed to protect electric loads and motors from failure and damage due to common current and voltage faults.

## Operating Principle

Relayne GSPT-MV constantly supervises current and voltage values; when any harmful condition occurs, the output connection is deactivated until the fault disappears, power line conditions returns to an acceptable level and the motor has been totally cooled. Specific timing such as Start Up Delay (TC) and Trip Delay (TD) are incorporated to prevent nuisance tripping due to rapid power fluctuations.

## Key Features

- **Total Protection for Submersible Pumps**: Limits the amount of allowed starting during continuous hours of operation according to the capacity of the motor given in HP power
- **Continuous Protection**: Protects continuously against current values, voltage values and events of Locked Rotor Accelerated
- **Adjustable Timing**: Provides adjustable timing to connection and after overload
- **Automatic Voltage Adaptation**: Automatically adapts the operational limits allowed of over voltage and under voltage according to nominal voltage of the lines
- **Multi-voltage Power Supply**: Contains a multivoltage power supply that automatically adapts itself to any nominal voltage
- **16x2 Backlight LCD Display**: Displays current values, voltage values, unbalance, frequency, load report information and events on screen
- **User Interface**: Four (4) push buttons (1 for START, 2 for ADJUST and 1 for SELECT) for operation and protection parameters adjustment
- **Communication Port**: Includes Communication Port with MODBUS RTU protocol

## Supported Voltages

**Standard Voltages**: 208, 220, 230, 240, 400, 440, 480 VAC

**Special Voltages**: 200, 420, 460 VAC

## Mechanical Design and Installation

The innovative mechanical design allows three (3) placement options:
- Symmetrical Din-Rail mounting
- Flat Surface mounting, using an exclusive attachable mounting ear
- Flush mounting (optional)

## Standards and Compliance

Relayne GSPT-MV has been developed using the most advanced technology and designed in accordance with the IEEE, IEC and NEMA protection standards and developed in compliance with IEC electromagnetic compatibility standards, working safely under hardest electrical environments.

## Safety Notice

Only qualified technicians with knowledge about overload protection relay and associated machinery should do the installation, starting up, and maintenance of the system. Failure to comply may result in equipment damage and/or personal injury.
