# Relayne GSPT - Submersible Pump Integral Protection Relay

## Features

Relayne GSPT is a microcontrolled based three-phase Integral Motor Protection Relay, specifically designed to protect electric loads and motors from failure and damage due to common current and voltage faults.

### Measurements
- Current
- Voltage
- Frequency
- Power Factor (PF), Reactive Power (KVA)
- Real Power (KW)
- Energy Consumption (KWH)
- Temperature

### Protection Against
- Overload / Undercurrent
- Overvoltage / Undervoltage
- Frequency Shift
- Voltage Unbalance
- Current Unbalance
- Single Phasing
- Phase Reversal
- Locked Rotor

### Adjustments
- Underload current
- Overvoltage
- Undervoltage
- Current Unbalance
- Voltage Unbalance
- Frequency
- Trip Delay
- Start Up Delay after Voltage Fault Recovery
- Motor Thermal Class
- Clock Adjustment
- Control of Motor High-Inertia Load
- Schedule timer

### Physical Features
- Din-Rail, Flat Surface or Flush mounting
- 16x2 LCD Display with current values, voltage values and load report information on screen

  - Flush mounting

## General Functions & Range of Applications (Intended Use)

Relayne GSPT constantly supervises current and voltage values; when any harmful condition occurs, the output connection is deactivated until the fault disappears, power line conditions return to an acceptable level and the motor has been totally cooled. Specific timing such as Start Up Delay (TC) and Trip Delay (TD) are incorporated to prevent nuisance tripping due to rapid power fluctuations.

Relayne GSPT increases protection for submersible pumps by limiting the amount of allowed starting during continuous hours of operation according to the motor capacity given in HP power. It protects continuously against events of Locked Rotor Accelerated and provides adjustable timing for connection after overload.

Relayne GSPT provides LCD Display to indicate the output status, voltage, current, unbalance, frequency and load status and failure conditions. It also provides four (4) push buttons for operation and protection parameters adjustment, and includes an output Communication Port with MODBUS RTU protocol.
