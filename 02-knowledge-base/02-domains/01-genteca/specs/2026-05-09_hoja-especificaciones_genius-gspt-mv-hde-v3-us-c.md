# GSPT-MV Submersible Pump Total Protection Relay

## Product Overview

Relayne GSPT-MV is a microcontrolled based three-phase Total Motor Protection Relay, specifically designed to protect electric loads and motors from failure and damage due to common current and voltage faults.

## Key Features

- **Continuous Supervision**: Relayne GSPT-MV constantly supervises current and voltage values; when any harmful condition occurs, the output connection is deactivated until the fault disappears, power line conditions return to an acceptable level and the motor has been totally cooled.

- **Protection for Submersible Pumps**: Limits the amount of allowed starting during continuous hours of operation according to the capacity of the motor given in HP power.

- **Protective Functions**:
  - Protects continuously against current values and voltage values
  - Locked Rotor protection with Accelerated detection
  - Adjustable timing for connection after overload
  - Automatically adapts operational limits for over-voltage and under-voltage protection according to nominal voltage of the lines

- **Timing Features**:
  - Start Up Delay (TC)
  - Trip Delay (TD)
  - Prevents nuisance tripping due to rapid power fluctuations

## Multivoltage Power Supply

A same voltage model operates in any of the following voltages:

**Standard Voltages**: 208, 220, 230, 240, 400, 440, 480 VAC

**Special Voltages**: 200, 420, 460 VAC

## Display and Controls

- 16x2 backlit LCD Display with current values, voltage values and events
- Load report information on screen
- Four (4) push buttons:
  - 1 for START
  - 2 for ADJUST
  - 1 for SELECT
- Communication Port with MODBUS RTU protocol

## Mounting Options

An innovative mechanical design allows three (3) placement options:

1. Symmetrical Din-Rail mounting
2. Flat Surface mounting, using an exclusive attachable mounting ear
3. Flush mounting (optional)

## Standards Compliance

Relayne GSPT-MV has been developed using advanced technology and designed in accordance with IEEE, IEC and NEMA protection standards. Developed in compliance with IEC electromagnetic compatibility standards, working safely under the hardest electrical environments.

## Materials

PC, ABS, NYLON

Only qualified technicians with knowledge about overload protection relay and associated machinery should perform the installation, starting up, and maintenance of the system. Failure to comply may result in equipment damage and/or personal injury.
