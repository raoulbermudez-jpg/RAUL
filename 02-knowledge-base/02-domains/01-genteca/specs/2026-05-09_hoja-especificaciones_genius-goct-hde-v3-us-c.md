## Overview

The Relayne GOCT has been developed using the most advanced technology and designed in accordance with the IEEE, IEC and NEMA protection standards. The relay has been developed in compliance with IEC electromagnetic compatibility standards, working safely under the hardest electrical environments.

The Relayne GOCT Motor Protection Relay provides an optimal solution for protecting important industrial investments through comprehensive electrical protection functions.

## General Characteristics

The Relayne GOCT provides electrical protection through general functions and setting ranges for:

- Motor protection
- Overload protection
- Cold-hot curve characteristics
- Start up delay setting knob (TC)
- Contact configuration (open/closed)
