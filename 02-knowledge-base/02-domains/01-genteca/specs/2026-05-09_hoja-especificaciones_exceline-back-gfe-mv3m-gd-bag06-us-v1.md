# Float Switch GFE-MV3M

## Installation Instructions

**IMPORTANT:** Disconnect the power source before installing.

### Preparation
- Drill a hole on the cover of the tank to allow the cable to pass through
- Put the float in the tank, adjusting the counterweight as described in the instructions
- Connect the cables
- Any non-used cables must be protected with electrical tape

## Adjustment of the Counterweight

### Steps
1. Remove the segmented ring from the flat side of the counterweight (A)
2. Insert the retainer ring on the cable on the desired position of the counterweight (B)
3. Insert the cable in the conic side of the counterweight. It will stop on the place where the retainer ring is (C)

## Modes of Use

### Mode 1 - Maximum Level Control
Use the BLUE and BLACK cables as an OFF SWITCH to turn-off the pump when the water reaches the maximum level.

### Mode 2 - Minimum Level Control
Use the BROWN and BLACK cables as an OFF SWITCH to turn-off the pump when the water reaches the minimum level.

## Technical Specifications

- **Voltage:** 120 / 220 V~ 50 / 60 Hz
- **Max. resistive current:** 16 A
- **Max. inductive current:** 4 A
- **Application:** For electric motors and water pumps up to 373 W / 0.5 HP
- **Operating temperature:** 32 °F to 131 °F (0 °C to 55 °C)

## Important Warnings

- FOR DIRECT USE in pumps up to 373 W / 0.5 HP
- FOR BIGGER PUMPS the use of a contactor is required
- Do not insert the cable into a conduit type pipe
- Do not exceed the maximum current capacity for your security and warranty purposes
