# GIII+MV Total Motor Protection Relay

## Overview

The GIII+MV is a microcontrolled based three-phase Total Motor Protection Relay, specifically designed to protect electric loads and motors from failure and damage due to common current and voltage faults.

GIII+MV constantly supervises current and voltage levels; when any harmful condition occurs, the output connection is deactivated until the fault disappears, the power line conditions returns to an acceptable level and the motor has been totally cooled. Specific timers as Start Up Delay (TC) and Trip Delay (TD) are incorporated to prevent nuisance tripping due to rapid power fluctuations.

The GIII+MV provides LCD Display to indicate the output status voltage, current, unbalance, frequency and load status and failure conditions. An innovative mechanical design allows three placement options:
- Symmetrical Din-Rail mounting
- Flat Surface mounting, using an exclusive attachable mounting ear
- Flush mounting
