IV. Switch for on (I) and off (O)

---

## Installation Instructions

1. Plug the appliance into the Exceline protector
2. Plug the Exceline protector into the socket
3. Turn on the protector's switch "I". If there isn't a voltage failure, the green light will start blinking during the time delay. After that, the light will stay on and connect the appliance to the power line. The time delay can be between 30 seconds and 3 minutes according to the internal protector's algorithm.

---

## Operation

### A. Voltage Failure Detection

When a voltage failure occurs, the protector will indicate the type of failure and disconnect the equipment to be protected.

**HIGH VOLTAGE DETECTION:** If the voltage exceeds the maximum value acceptable, the red light of the High Voltage indicator will turn on and the protected equipment will turn off.

**LOW VOLTAGE DETECTION:** If the voltage is below the minimum voltage acceptable, the red light of the Low Voltage indicator will turn on and the protected equipment will turn off.

### B. Normal Voltage Conditions

When the voltage is in normal conditions, the yellow LED will turn on until a timer of 3 minutes is completed. If the equipment has been off more than 3 minutes, the waiting timer will be 30 seconds.

### C. Normal Operation

Subsequently, the NORMAL VOLTAGE indicator (green LED light) will turn on and the protector will connect the load.

### D. Multiple Protector Installation

The protector is designed so that, if there is more than one protector installed, they will not turn on simultaneously. This small random variation between them will give you great advantages.
